About Us

In the forever changing world which constantly requires modern solutions, a group of young people driven by the spirit of entrepreneurship and innovation established a student accommodation agency Student Connect Pty Ltd which operates as an agent between the student and the landlord. Student Connect (SC) also acts as agent between the landlord and the university. Student Connect plays an effective role in managing and administrating the privately owned accommodation on behalf of the landlord. Before managing the property we ensure that the property meets the standards stipulated in the NSFAS criteria and/or the University criteria for Private accommodations. All student accommodation managed by SC Student Accommodation Agency are privately owned.

Student Connect identified the gap and saw an opportunity in the market that allowed SC to develop a very specific service that brings together the landlords (owners of properties) with students looking for accommodation. SC acts on behalf of both parties to ensure the wellbeing of all involved parties in the leasing process. This has provided the company with a unique capability enabling SC to facilitate almost any type of accommodation request. Regardless of your budget, housing requirements or other preferences, SC will be able to find you the perfect place where you feel at home away from home for the students and ensure that the property is in good condition with the provision of proper services for the landlord, because SC is your only student accommodation solution. When your house becomes your home you are able to relax and pay attention to what really matters: Focusing on your academics and preparing for your future.

SC involvement does not stop once a student has been placed at a preferred accommodation. The company provides a continuous solution to student accommodation, including management, administration, transport, marketing, maintenance, gardening, and cleaning services and a 24-hour helpline. This creates some peace of mind – not only for students, but for their parents, guardians as well as for the landlords of the properties.

About Us

Student Connect identified the gap in the market that allowed SC to develop a very specific service that brings together the landlords (owners of properties) with students looking for accommodation. In the forever changing world, which constantly requires modern solutions, a group of young people driven by the spirit of entrepreneurship and innovation established the Agency Student Connect (PTY) LTD.


Student Connect Pty Ltd operates as an agent between the student and the landlord (property owner). Student Connect (SC) is owned and managed by a group of active youths who are passionate about student life and easing the life of property owners. Student Connect (SC) also acts paves way for the landlord as they mediate with the university on property owners. Student Connect plays an effective role in making sure the property does meet the stipulated NFSAS/University Criteria. Student Connect provides a continuous solution to student accommodation, including management, administration, transport, marketing, maintenance, gardening, Internet and Security installation services, and cleaning services and a 24-hour helpline.
